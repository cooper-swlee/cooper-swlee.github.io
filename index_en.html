Our Love Story

Unforgettable 3 hours in the other side of the world

“Uh, Are you Korean?(In Korean) ¿Eres Coreano?”
Who knew they would meet their future spouses there, which takes about 27 hours from South Korea.

They wandered around the downtown of Cusco, walked by narrow alleys to the prospect and enjoyed their moments. Could it be that the goddess of Inca mythology used magic?

They, who had gotten interested in each other after spending a short time there, met up again in Seoul, South Korea and fell in love with each other. After precious 3 years together, finally they want to swear lifelong love.
